Launch Event of Caballo Loco Art Gallery

Our launch event promises to be an unforgettable experience filled with excitement, creativity, and camaraderie. Here's what you can expect:

  • Art Exhibition: Immerse yourself in a world of equine-inspired artistry as you explore our gallery's diverse collection of sculptures, paintings, and crafts.
  • Live Demonstrations: Witness the magic of creation as talented artisans demonstrate their craft live, giving you a glimpse into the intricate process behind each masterpiece.
  • Interactive Workshops: Unleash your own creativity with hands-on workshops where you can try your hand at sculpting, painting, and other horse-themed crafts under the guidance of experienced artists.
  • Special Guests: Rub shoulders with esteemed guests from the equestrian and art communities, including renowned artists, horse enthusiasts, and advocates for animal welfare.
  • Culinary Delights: Indulge your senses with a delectable selection of gourmet treats and refreshments inspired by the elegance and sophistication of equestrian culture, meticulously prepared by our culinary artisans.
