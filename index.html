Why Choose Polo Cafe

At Polo Cafe, we think a fabulous cafe is not just about coffee, it's about making an experience good enough to return for. Strategically placed on Golf Course Road in Sector 56, Gurugram, we're accessible to friends, families, and working professionals alike. Our skilled baristas prepare each cup with love, from our indulgent, creamy cappuccino to our forever-favorite, delightfully chilled cold coffee. Combine your drink with one of our comforting sandwiches or decadent shakes, all crafted fresh to curb every craving. With hidden nooks for solo work, cozy seating for group reunions, and a friendly, inviting atmosphere, Polo Cafe isn't a cafe, it's a community hotspot relied upon by locals for more than seven years.
